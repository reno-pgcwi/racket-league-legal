Privacy Policy

Racket League · Last updated: 9 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PGCWI ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Racket League mobile application (the "App"). We are based in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. By creating an account and using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

We are committed to handling your personal information in accordance with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and applicable privacy laws in the regions where our users live, including the United States.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information that is needed to run the App. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use third-party advertising or analytics tracking SDKs.

Information you provide

DataWhy we collect it
Email addressTo create and secure your account and sign you in.
First and last name (and optional display name)To identify you to other members of your groups.
Phone number (optional)If you choose to provide it, to help group organizers reach you.

Sports, skill levels, and match resultsTo organize play, track scores, and show statistics.
Messages, posts, and trash-talk contentTo deliver chat and social features within your groups.

Information collected automatically

DataWhy we collect it

Basic technical/diagnostic dataTo keep the App running reliably and fix errors. We do not use this to track you across other apps or websites.

Device permissions

  • Photos / Camera - only when you choose to set a profile picture.
  • Calendar - only if you opt in to adding your matches to your device calendar.
  • Notifications - only if you allow them, to send match and group updates.
  • Microphone - requested by a component dependency; only used if you actively record within a feature that needs it.

You can change or revoke any of these permissions at any time in your device settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the App's features.
  • To organize groups, series, matches, and scoring.
  • To deliver notifications and invites you've opted into.
  • To keep the App secure and prevent abuse.
  • To respond to your support requests.

We do not use your personal information for advertising, and we do not sell or rent it to anyone.

3. Who Can See Your Information

The App is a social tool, so some information is shared with other users by design:

  • Your name, display name, profile photo, and stats are visible to members of groups you join.
  • Messages and posts you create are visible to the intended recipients (a group, a thread, or a direct-message partner).
  • If you set your profile to public, your profile may be visible more broadly within the App. You control this in your settings.

We never share your information publicly outside the App or with advertisers.

5. International Data Transfers

We are based in Canada, and our users include people in the United States. Your information may be stored and processed in Canada, the United States, or other countries where our service providers operate. By using the App, you consent to your information being transferred to and processed in these locations, which may have different data-protection laws than your own. We take reasonable steps to ensure your information remains protected wherever it is processed.

6. Data Retention

We keep your personal information for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we delete your personal profile information and associated personal data. Certain content may be retained in anonymized or de-identified form (for example, historical match scores within a group are kept without identifying you) so that group records remain consistent. We may also retain limited information where required to comply with legal obligations or resolve disputes.

7. Deleting Your Account

You can permanently delete your account at any time from Settings → Delete Account inside the App. This removes your profile and associated personal data as described above. If you have trouble deleting your account, contact us at reno@pgcwi.com and we will process your request.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you have rights over your personal information, including:

  • The right to access the personal information we hold about you.
  • The right to correct inaccurate information (you can edit most of it directly in the App).
  • The right to delete your account and personal information.
  • The right to withdraw consent for optional data and notifications.

Canadian users: Under PIPEDA you may request access to your personal information and ask us to correct it. US users (including residents of states with privacy laws such as California): you have the right to know what we collect, to request deletion, and not to be discriminated against for exercising these rights. We do not sell personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, email reno@pgcwi.com.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

10. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections and access controls at the database level. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your information and limit access to it.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above and, for significant changes, notify you within the App. Your continued use of the App after changes take effect means you accept the updated policy.
